# NYC TAXI TRIP DURATION PREDICTION
- Used linear regression, decision tree, random forest, xgboost, stacking and averaging to predict duration of taxi trip
- Achieved Root Mean Squared Error(rmse) of 0.62 for training and 0.62 for validation for linear regression
- Achieved rmse of 0.38 for training and 0.43 for validation for decision tree
- Achieved rmse of 0.41 for training and 0.43 for validation for random forest
- Achieved rmse of 0.38 for training and 0.41 for validation for best iteration of xgboost
- Achieved rmse of 0.39 for training and 0.42 for validation for stacking xgboost, random forest and decision tree
- Achieved rmse of 0.41 for validation for averaging on random forest and xgboost
- Used - Python, seaborn, sklearn
